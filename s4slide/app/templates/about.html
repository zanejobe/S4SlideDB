The funding for creation of this website was provided by a Earth Science Information Partners (ESIP) grant to Zane Jobe (view the grant application here). The front-end and back-end portions of the website were developed at Colorado School of Mines by industrious computer-science students (alphabetically) Brianna Gaede, Eric Klatzco, Lucas Kitaev, Jessy Liao, Vlad Muresan, with minimal oversight by Zane Jobe. {% load static %}

The impetus for the creation of this site was the 2nd Workshop on Subaqueous Landslides and Morphometric Parameters in June 2019 in Golden, CO. A large group of people (see the list here) worked to form a proposal that was funded by ESIP.

The format of the database follows that of the peer-reviewed article "A consistent global approach for the morphometric characterization of subaqueous landslides" by Mike Clare and co-authors. This paper is open-access and can be downloaded from the link above, or in PDF format here.
